Frequently Asked Questions

What materials can I put in a UDS dumpster?

Our dumpsters accept general household items, furniture, construction materials, yard waste, and more. Hazardous materials like paint, chemicals, and asbestos are not allowed.

How long can I keep the dumpster?

Standard rental periods last 7 days, but we offer flexible extensions based on your project timeline.

Can I put appliances in a dumpster?

Small appliances are typically fine, but refrigerators, air conditioners, and anything containing Freon are not permitted. Our team can advise on proper disposal.

Can the dumpster be placed on a city street in front of my business?

In most municipalities, placing a roll off dumpster bin on a public street requires a permit from the city's department of public works or a similar entity. You, as the renter, are typically responsible for securing this permit before we can schedule the delivery.

How much advance notice do you typically need to schedule a dumpster rental?

To ensure we can provide you with your preferred size on your desired date, we recommend calling at least 24 to 48 hours in advance. However, if you're in a bind, call us! We can often accommodate same-day or next-day requests for a roll off rental near me.

What happens if I need to keep the dumpster longer than expected?

Extensions are available. Just call our office, and we’ll adjust the rental period to fit your timeline.
